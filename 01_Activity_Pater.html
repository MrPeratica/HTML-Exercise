ORIGINAL PATER

INGREDIENTS

  • ½ cup Canola Oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic minced
  • 1 med. Red Onion minced
  • 350 g Chicken boiled & Shredded
  • 4 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 3 tbsp White Vinegar
  • 1½ tbsp White/Brown Sugar
  • ½ tsp Black Pepper
  • ½ tsp Turmeric Powder optional
  • 2 tbsp Margarine optional

PROCEDURE

~ Shredded Chicken ~

  1. In a pot add 1 liter of water, 1 tsp Salt and 1 tsp Black Pepper & 2 Laurel Leaves
  2. Add 350g Chicken Breast Fillet
  3. Boil on med. heat for about 15 mins.
  4. Drain, let it cool down & shred the chicken

~ Let's Fry ~

  1. In a fry pan pour ½ cup Oil
  2. Add minced Garlic and minced Red Onion
  3. Fry until translucent then add 350g shredded chicken and saute
  4. Add ½tsp Black Pepper, ½tsp Turmeric & 1½tbsp Sugar
  5. Add 4 tbsp soy sauce & saute
  6. Add 3 tbsp vinegar & let it simmer
  7. add 2 tbsp margarine (optional)
  8. Fry until chicken flakes are crispy

~ Assemble ~

  1. Originally Pastil is wrapped in Banana Leaves.
  2. But I'm using a 400ml Plastic Container at the moment.
  3. 1/2 cup of Rice, Lettuce (optional), Tomato,
  4. hard boild Egg & 1/4 cup of Pastil
  5. You can also add Cucumber

Serve and Enjoy!
